The plan

By image, Zalgo text can become VERY hard to read, and Sean thought, if an algorithn could flawlessly detect these characters, know what they meant, what they do, recognize and display the symbols it portrays, it could be a step to make the perfect detection algorithm.

So now there is a project on SNU where the community can play with Zalgo text and create the perfect algorithm. We are doing it weekly, and doing a different difficulty of text daily.

For this project, SNU is giving out rewards, 2 xp for every byte, and 1 platinum for every discovery. Big discoveries and exploit can give money rewards up to $1000 USD
